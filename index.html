JNTUK-UCEV

The JNTUK University College of Engineering Vizianagaram was established in the academic year 2007-08 with five Under Graduate Engineering Courses Viz., B.Tech. In EEE, ECE, CSE, ME, IT, CIVIL and METALLURGY. Now it is a well established constituent college under JNTU Kakinada. Situated amidst an ambient location, the college has a very fast-developing infrastructure.

ITYUKTA

ITYUKTA is a technical fest organised by the Department of Information Technology, JNTUK-UCEV Vizianagaram.
It encompasses knowledgable workshops and a variety of technical and non technical events where students can learn and gain a different experience which provides every individual a platform to explore one's talent.

INTRO/PROMO

This year there are four workshops where students can enhance their knowledge and get to know how to implement the particular subject in real time. There are also few technical events like Code Buddy, PPT presentation, Spped Typing, Online Teasure Hunt and Squash the Code.

A Guide To BlockChain EcoSystem

Workshop Details.

Blockchain is the backbone Technology of Digital CryptoCurrency BitCoin. The blockchain is a distributed database of records of all transactions or digital event that have been executed and shared among participating parties. A blockchain, is a growing list of records, called blocks, that are linked using cryptography.
